About BrainBin

We are a team of passionate engineers and innovators dedicated to solving the world's waste management challenges. Our Smart Waste Management System is the result of countless hours of research, design, and development, aiming to make recycling smarter and more sustainable.

The Project

This Smart Waste Management System classifies and sorts waste into four compartments: Dry, Wet, Metal, and Unknown. The system uses a combination of advanced sensors and machine vision to detect and actuate sorting paths.

Hardware & Technology

  • Microcontrollers: Arduino Mega 2560, ESP32-S3 AI camera
  • Sensors: Proximity, IR, break-beam, ultrasonic, weight, moisture
  • Actuators: Vibration motor, servo, stepper motor
  • Power: Solar panel
