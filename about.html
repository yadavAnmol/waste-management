About the Project

This Smart Waste Management System is designed to automate the segregation of household and commercial waste, promoting efficient recycling and a cleaner environment. By combining cutting-edge sensors and machine vision, our system provides a reliable and autonomous sorting solution.

Hardware

  • **Microcontrollers:** Arduino Mega 2560, ESP32-S3 AI camera
  • **Sensors:** Proximity, IR, break-beam, ultrasonic, weight, moisture
  • **Actuators:** Vibration motor, servo, stepper motor
  • **Power:** Solar panel
  • **Mechanical:** Conveyor belt kit, hopper

How It Works

  1. **Entry:** Items are placed into the hopper and enter the system via the conveyor.
  2. **Classification:** A combination of sensors and the **ESP32-S3 AI camera** classify the waste into Dry, Wet, Metal, or Unknown.
  3. **Sorting:** Motors actuate the sorting gates, directing the item to the correct bin.
  4. **Reporting:** The system updates the dashboard with real-time data on bin levels and waste distribution.
