Automated sorting, smarter recycling.

Our Smart Waste Management System uses advanced sensors, motors, and an **ESP32-S3 AI camera** to efficiently classify and sort waste into four categories: Dry, Wet, Metal, and Unknown. The system is powered by solar energy and controlled by an **Arduino Mega 2560**, making it an ideal solution for remote and sustainable waste management.

Key Components

Sensors & AI Vision

Proximity, IR, break-beam, ultrasonic, weight, and moisture sensors work in tandem with the **ESP32-S3 AI camera** for robust waste detection and classification.

Sorting Actuators

Our system uses a combination of vibration, servo, and stepper motors to accurately drive the sorting mechanisms and conveyor belt.

Control & Power

The entire operation is controlled by the **Arduino Mega 2560** and powered by a solar panel, ensuring reliability and energy independence.
